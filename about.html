hi, from xhilda

Flowertales is a reflection of my love for flowers—a space where blooms tell stories, where you can learn to create your own arrangements, and where a community of floral enthusiasts comes together. I grew up in a house surrounded by a vibrant garden, where flowers were not just plants but companions. Since childhood, I have been crafting small bouquets, tending to petals, and even whispering to flowers. This website is an extension of that passion—an invitation to explore, create, and let flowers speak in your own way.
