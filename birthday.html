step by step guide

01.
think playful & personal

Choose bright, happy flowers that match the recipient’s personality—cheerful sunflowers, colorful gerberas, or fragrant lilies. Add something special they love!

02.
prep time

Using clean scissors or floral shears, cut the stems at a 45° angle to help them drink water. Strip off any leaves that will be below water and let them rest in a vase.

03.
arrange with joy

Begin with your boldest flowers in the center and fan out smaller blooms and filler around them (like waxflower, statice, or baby’s breath).

04.
add textures & surprise

Place in a few unexpected elements like berries or ribbon pieces. To make it more fun you can include even fruits or tiny decorations that match with the vibe.

05.
wrap in celebration

Use colorful wrapping paper or soft tulle. For a layered look, you can stack two pieces diagonally like a diamond shape. Lay your bouquet diagonally on the paper, fold the bottom corner up over the stems, then wrap one side across and overlap with the other.

06.
final touch

Tie it off with ribbon just below the blooms to add structure. Include personal touches like a note card or even varied textures for playfulness.

tools and materials

Before you start, make sure you have everything you need within reach.
These will help you bring your vision to life with ease and flow.

For this composition you will need:

-sharp scissors of floral shears
-string for tying and ribbon
-tissue paper for wrapping
